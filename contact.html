Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical residential project takes 3-6 months, while larger commercial projects may take 6-12 months or more.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote meetings or are located outside our immediate service area.

What is included in your design services?

Our services include concept development, space planning, material selection, furniture specification, project management, and post-completion support.

Do you work with specific budgets?

We work with various budgets and can tailor our services to meet your financial requirements while maintaining quality and design integrity.
